FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about our Private Docker Registry

Is there a free trial?

Yes, the first 14 days are free. On the 15th day your Credit will be charged. During the trial period you can cancel the service without any costs on your side.

Can the Private Docker Registry be installed in my Datacenter?

We can also setup the Private Docker Registry on premise in Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS, Google Compute Engine, OpenShift, Kubernetes and other IaaS/PaaS service provider upon request. Contact us to request more details regarding the setup process.

Can I change the Mode of Payment?

We currently have only one subscription plan, but we preparing additional subscription plans that will be eligible for a change any time.

We have a special setup in our Organisation can you help us?

Most of our customers especially large enterprises have special requirements. We created many tailor made Docker Registry integrations and customizations. Contact us to find our more.

Which payment options do I have?

We accept DebitDirect and Credit card payments from Visa, MasterCard and American Express.

Is it safe to share credit card details with you?

We are working together only with PCI-DSS compliant payment service providers Stripe, GoCardless and Chargebee with an excellent reputation, auditing and compliance. At no time and under no circumstances we or our employees are able to reveal your payment details. Your payment details are always handled by the above payment service providers directly.
