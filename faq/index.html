Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs about our Container Registry Service

How is the Free Trial working?

Your first 14 days are completely on us. During the trial period you can cancel the service anytime at no cost. On the 15th day your credit card will be charged according to your selected plan.

How can I manage the Subscription?

Every aspect of your subscription like subscription tier, payment method, billing cycle, billing details and cancellation can be Manged by yourself in the Customer Portal.

How can I change the Payment Method?

We currently only offer a single payment method. In the near future we will also offer DebitDirect payment options and You will be able to switch the payment method in your Customer Portal.

Which payment options do you provide?

Currently, we accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express Credit Cards. In the near future we will also offer DebitDirect payment options.

Is it safe to share Credit Card details with you?

We only work with PCI-DSS compliant payment service providers like Stripe, GoCardless and Billby that all have an excellent reputation, auditing and compliance. At no time and under no circumstances are we capable of viewing your credit card information. Your credit card details are always processed directly by the above mentioned payment service providers and never by us.

How to Cancel your Service?

Canceling our service is as easy as signing up. We don't have any shady Mail or Fax roadblocks that makes it hard for you to cancel our service. Simply go to your Customer Portal and just click on cancel subscription, that's it.

Can your Container Registry also run in our Datacenter?

We can also set up and mange the Container Registry as a managed service for you on Azure, Amazon AWS, Google Compute Engine, OpenShift, Kubernetes and any other IaaS/PaaS upon request. Contact us for more details on the setup process.

We have a special requirement in our Organization can you help us?

Most of our customers especially large enterprises have special requirements. We provide you with tailor made Container Registry integrations and customizations options. Contact us to work out a solution to address your needs

Where are my Container Images Stored and Processed?

Our data center are located in Paris France and Amsterdam Netherlands.

Where are your Data Center?

Our Container Registry data centers is currently located in Paris France and Amsterdam Netherlands. We plan to expand our locations to North America and Asia in the near future.
