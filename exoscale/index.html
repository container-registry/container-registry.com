Get Started

Simple and Transparent pay-as-you-go pricing

Container Registry on Exoscale has a simple pricing model, you only pay for consumed storage space. Payment is made through the Exoscale account.

Service Use

Only Exoscale customers can subscribe and use the manged Container Registry Service on Exoscale exo.container-registry.com.

Sign Up

Container Registry on Exoscale subscription is activated on the Exoscale Marketplace.

User Onboarding

Two options exist to onboard users from your organization to Container Registry:

  1. users with Exoscale portal access will automatically receive an email invitation to Container Registry as well as permission to your organization’s private project area.
  2. users without Exoscale portal access can register themselves on Container Registry but have no initial access to the private project area of your organization. Project admins can invite these users via the Container Registry Web UI.
