Main Features

Managed Service

Get a dedicated Container Registry exclusively for your organization that is hosted on a dedicated cluster isolated from other tenants with guaranteed bandwidth and processing power.

Authentication and Authorization

Enterprise grade authentication with single sign-on for LDAP, Microsoft Active Directory and OAuth/OpenID Connect. Fine-grained authorization capabilities with role-based access control on user, team and namespace level make sure you are always in control.

Vulnerability Scanner

Regular vulnerability scans with Trivy or Clair in combination with policy checks prevent that vulnerable images reach your production environments.

Commercial Image Distribution

Distribute your commercial or closed source images exclusively and securely with your customers and grand them access to easily pull specific images during a specific period of time.

Security

All traffic is secured with SSL/TLS. Container Images are stored encrypted on disks

Data Protection

All data is stored and processed by us in European data centers in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Data centers in North America will be available in the near future.

Data Durability

Triple image replication data durability means that your images are always available.
