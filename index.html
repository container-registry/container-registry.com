Make your Container Centric Workflows a breeze

We offer a Container Registry that supports various CI/CD workflow and is not only a dump bucket you get from Azure, AWS or GCP.
Invest in a proper Container Registry to ensure that images are screened and scanned for vulnerabilities before reaching your Servers. With the capability to create rules, you can prevent the deployment of vulnerable images or enforce Immutability on image tags, so once pushed, images can’t be overwritten, deleted, or altered. Predictable costs without bandwidth fees and retention rules with continuous cleanup are part of the service we offer and will keep your registry in autopilot mode. This makes us a red hot AWS ECR alternative.

Main Features

Managed Service

As part of your subscription, you will get a dedicated Container Registry exclusively for your organization that is hosted on a dedicated cluster isolated from other tenants with guaranteed bandwidth and processing power. Always.

Authentication and Authorization

Benefit from an enterprise-grade authentication with single sign-on for LDAP, Microsoft Active Directory, and OAuth/OpenID Connect. On top of this, Fine-grained authorization capabilities with role-based access control on user, team, and namespace level make sure you are in control. Always

Vulnerability Scanner

Feel right at home, with regular vulnerability scans with Trivy or Clair in combination with policy checks that prevent vulnerable images from reaching your production environments. Always.

Commercial Image Distribution

Easily distribute your commercial or closed source Containerized Software with your customers and grant them access to directly pull specific images during a specific period. Always.

Security

All traffic is secured with SSL/TLS. Container Images are stored encrypted on disks to ensure your privacy is paramount. Always.

Data Protection

All data is stored and processed in European data centers as per the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Data centers in North America will be available soon. We will look into these opportunities as they become available.

Data Durability

Have trouble trusting your servers? Triple image replication and data durability means that your images are always available. Always.
