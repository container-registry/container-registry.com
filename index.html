Make your Container Centric Workflows a breeze

Finally a Container Registry that supports your workflow and isn't just a dump bucket. Ensure images are trusted and scanned for vulnerabilities before reaching production. Periodic checks prevent vulnerable images from deployment. Immutability rules enforce that image tags once pushed can't be overwritten, deleted or altered. Main Features

Dedicated Hosting

Your get a dedicated Container Registry alone for your organization hosted on a dedicated cluster shielded from other tenants with guaranteed bandwidth and processing power.

AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION

Container Registry with built in Enterprise grade authentication and single sign-on for LDAP, Microsoft Active Directory or OAuth/OpenID. Management and fine grained role-based access control on user, team and namespace level.

VULNERABILITY SCANNER

Each image is automatically scanned for vulnerabilities.

COMMERCIAL IMAGE DISTRIBUTION

Distribute your commercial or closed source images exclusively and securely with your customers and grand them access to easily pull specific images during a specific period of time.

SECURITY

All traffic is secured with SSL/TLS. Container Images are stored encrypted on our side with AES. You also get a SSL/TLS certificate for free that is issued for your company’s domain name.

DATA PROTECTION

All data is stored and processed by us in European data centers in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Data centers in North America will be available in the near future.

DATA DURABILITY

Triple image replication and a 100% data durability means that your images are always available.
