Why a Dedicated Container Registry Service?

You can combine the advantages of a big cloud, open-source technology, and dedicated solution. Our managed service starts with capital letters since it differs drastically from its competitors of all kinds and sizes.

Container Registry enables you to trace back all changes in an image and match them with a user.

Auditability
